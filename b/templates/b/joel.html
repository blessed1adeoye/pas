Drama Team - Rm.1:10-20; Jer.18:2-6

2 Tim 3:16-17 states, “All Scripture is given by inspiration of God, and is profitable for doctrine, for reproof, for correction, for instruction in righteousness, that the man of God may be complete, thoroughly equipped for every good work”.

In line with the above verse of scripture, alongside with the divine mandate of this commission, The Joel Company of this great assembly, propagate the gospel through drama to liberate worshipers from the oppressions of the devil.

The mission of this team is to use theater as a ministry to encourage, instruct, and uplift the body of Christ and in the process, build up a vibrant and enthusiastic group of young people who are on fire for God.

Members of this department are completely sold-out to God and seek to glorify God through the performance ministry; in a manner that will edify God's people in and phenomenal, unique, and unforgettable way.

Their ministrations are scriptural-based, explore a variety of themes and cover most genres of theater including comedy, poetry, suspense, song and dance.

Membership is strictly by application. If you like drama and theater and/or have a natural flare for it, It's time to pray about joining this minstry. If you thereafter feel that God is leading you to serve Him in this unique ministry, please fill the online form and we will go from there...
