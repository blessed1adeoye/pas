The Prayer Force

The prayer team forms a formidable force within the Church. They are responsible for the continuous spiritual strengthening of the church's backbone through effective fervent prayer sessions consistently held everyday from 8:00am-9:30pm; and from 11:00pm to 2:00am on the third Friday of every month. The objectives of the prayer team are: To intercede for Winners Chapel International Worldwide
To intercede for the leadership of Winners Chapel International Worldwide
To intercede for the unsaved to come to the kingdom of God
To intercede for the leadership of the nation
To intercede for Gods guidance and direction for the Church and Nation

At the Prayer Force. we have many opportunities for you to join our Team. Whether you like to pray in a group, or pray in your own home, there ia a place for you.
